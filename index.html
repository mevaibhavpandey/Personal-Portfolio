About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineering student with a strong orientation towards defence technology, aerospace systems and real-time monitoring platforms.

I combine software engineering fundamentals with defence domain understanding, focusing on building systems inspired by real-world command, control and surveillance architectures used in modern defence environments.

My core interests include Defence Software Systems, Command & Control Platforms, Maritime Surveillance Systems, Aerospace Guidance & Simulation, Geospatial Intelligence Systems, Real-time Monitoring & Alert Systems and AI-assisted Threat Detection.

With national-level defence selection achievements (AIR 31 in NDA and AIR 50 in TES), I bring high-pressure performance capability, structured thinking, and strong discipline to my engineering approach. I focus on building systems that are operationally realistic, data-driven, scalable, reliable under stress and security-conscious.

My long-term vision is to work at the intersection of Defence Technology, Aerospace Systems, AI-Driven Monitoring Platforms, and National Security Software Systems—building domain-driven engineering systems inspired by real-world defence infrastructure and operational decision-making environments.
